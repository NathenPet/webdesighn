Mac and Cheese

Ingrediants

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Milk
  • Half and half
  • Shredded Chedder cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Cook the macaroni
  3. Make the roux (melt butter in a sauce pan over medium heat. Blend in floue, salt, pepper. cook for 2 minutes)
  4. Add milk and cheese
  5. Pour into baking dish
